Spanish Dish

Seafood Paella

This dish is a Spanish delicacy that is loaded with seafood favorites that include mussels, clams, and shrimp. Infused with a rich saffron scent, this dish is loaded with the flavors of Spanish cuisine that is sure to make your mouth water. To enhance its best flavors, cook it on a grill in a large skillet to distribute the flavors evenly.

Japan Dish

Nigiri

Nigiri is a type of sushi local to Japan, that is made up of an oval shaped mound of rice with a slice of raw fish pressed on top. Various types of fish are used for Nigiri sushi, like tuna, salmon, and albacore. Like other sushi, the process of making the dish requires steady patience and good ingredients. If done well, this dish is a delicacy that is absolutely divine, perfectly balancing the sweet and salty ingredients to create an amazing taste.

South African Dish

Bobotie

Bobotie or bobotjie is a South African classic that is loved by many in South Africa and across the world. It is a deep-dish casserole made from lamb or ground beef, various herbs and spices, curry powder, fruits, and a delectable golden custard. This dish is the epitome of sweet and savory, quickly becoming popular, being served as the main dish for various festivals and celebrations.

Italian Dish

Bruschetta

Bruschetta is an Italian classic that is loved by many all over the world. This appetizer features ripened tomatoes, fresh basil, garlic, and olive oil on toasted Italian or French bread. It is a simple dish that is easy to love, bringing in the freshness of summer with every bite.

Indonesia Dish

Nasi Goreng

Nasi Goreng, the national food of Indonesia, is often described as the Indonesian rendition of fried rice. This fried rice delicacy features a rich, spicy flavor that stands out from other varieties of its kind. It is a simple dish that includes precooked rice that is fried with a dash of oil with chopped shallots, garlic, chili, and ginger. To counteract the heavy savory flavor, sweet soy sauce is added generously, creating a deep flavor that is addictive.

German Dish

Schnitzel

Schnitzel is a German favorite that many love for its crunchy and fresh flavor. It features a boneless piece of meat that is pounded down to a thin slice, which is then crumb coated and fried to perfection. Though often served plain with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice, it can also be paired with a creamy sauce, mashed potatoes, or a fresh, herb-y salad.
