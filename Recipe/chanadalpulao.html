ChanaDal Pulao

Key Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati Rice, rinsed
  • 1/2 cup Split Chickpeas (Chana dal), rinsed & soaked for 1 hour in hot water
  • 2 tablespoon Ghee or Oil
  • 1/2 cup Onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Ginger, grated
  • 1 1/2 cups Water
  • 2 teaspoon Lime juice
  • Cilantro leaves, chopped

    • For Spices

  • 1/4 teaspoon Ground Turmeric (Haldi powder)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garam Masala
  • 1 teaspoon Salt, adjust to taste

    • Whole Spices

  • 2 Bay leaf (Tej Patta)
  • 3 Green Cardamom (Elaichi)
  • 5 Cloves (Laung), (optional)
  • 1 inch Cinnamon (Dalchini)
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera)
  • 2 Dried Red Chili Whole

Cooking Steps

  • Step 1: Rinse the Chana Dal and soak for 1 hour in hot water. You can also soak in regular tap water for 4 hours.

    • Instant Pot Method

  • Step 1: Start the instant pot in SAUTE mode and heat it. Add ghee and all the whole spices. SautÃ© them for 30 seconds.
  • Step 2: Add the onions, ginger and garlic. SautÃ© for 3-4 minutes until the onions are translucent.
  • Step 3: Add the rinsed basmati rice to the pot. Drain the water from the soaked Chana Dal and add it to the pot. Then add all the spices.
  • Step 4:
  • Step 5: Add water and give it a good stir. Press Cancel and close the instant pot lid with vent in sealing position.
  • Step 6:Set the instant pot to RICE mode for 12 mins (low pressure). When the instant pot beeps, quick release the pressure manually.
  • Step 7: Add lime juice, and fluff the rice. Garnish with cilantro and Chana Dal Pulao is ready to be served.

    • StoveTop Pressure Cooker Method

  • Step 1: For stovetop pressure cooker, add 2 cups of water and cook for 2 whistles. Let the pressure release naturally.

    • Stovetop Method

  • Step 1: Follow the same steps as in the above method until adding water on medium-high flame.
  • Step 2: Add 2 Â¼ cups of water for this method. Then let the ingredients come to a boil. Now simmer to low flame and cover the pot with a lid. Cook until the water is completely absorbed.
  • Step 3: The Chana Dal Pulao is ready to be enjoyed. Sprinkle lime juice and garnish with cilantro.
