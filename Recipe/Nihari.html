Chicken Nihari

Key Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Skinless chicken 16 pcs
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1⁄2 cup wheat flour
  • 1⁄2 packet nehari masala Shan
  • 1 tbsp homemade nihari masala recipe follows
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 3 1⁄8 cups water 4 tbsp
  • 1⁄2 cup ghee
  • 1⁄4 cup oil
  • salt to taste
  • cilantro for garnish chopped
  • lime/lemon wedges
  • green chilis for serving sliced

    • Homemade Masala Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp dry ginger powder
  • 10 cloves pepli
  • 4-6 pcs cloves
  • 1 stick cinammon
  • 2 tbsp mace
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1 pod star anise
  • 1 pod black cardamon
  • 2 tbsp fennel seeds

Cooking Steps

    Homemade Nihari Masala

  • Step 1: In a pan roast all the whole spices for a few minutes until fragrant.
  • Step 2: When cool, grind all the spiced in a spice grinder until smooth. Now mix in the powdered spices.
  • Step 3: Store in a glass jar.

    • Prepare Nihari

  • Step 1: In a bowl mix paprika and Shan nihari masala with 4 tbsp water and make a paste.
  • Step 2: In a bowl mix ½ cup flour with ¾ cup of water and make a smooth paste.
  • Step 3: In a heavy bottom stock pot heat oil and ghee.
  • Step 4: To this add the masala paste and cook for 30 seconds.
  • Step 5: Now add the chicken and fry for a couple of minutes.
  • Step 6: At this time add your ginger garlic paste, salt to taste and 1 tbsp homemade nihari masala and fry another few minutes on a medium low flame.
  • Step 7: Now add 3 cups of water and let it come to a boil.
  • Step 8: Add this point add your wheat flour paste, stirring continuously to avoid lumps. You will notice that the oil will disappear.
  • Step 9: Turn down the flame to medium, cover the pot and let it simmer for 30 minutes until the chicken is completely cooked.
  • Step 10: At this point you will notice the oil surfacing around the rim of the pot. Your chicken nihari is now ready.
  • Step 11: To serve sprinkle with cilantro, sliced green chili and lime/lemon wedges.
  • Step 12: Enjoy with hot naan!
