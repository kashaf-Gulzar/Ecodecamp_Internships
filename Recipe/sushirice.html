Sushi Rice

Key Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 medium onion
  • 4 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 tablespoons mirin kelp, leaf (optional)

Cooking Steps

  • Step 1: Wash and rinse the rice till the water runs clear and soak it for a minimum of 30 minutes. (see notes)
  • Step 2: Wash and prep Chicken if it needs to be cut and/or skinned.
  • Step 3: Heat the oil and add the sliced onions, cooking till they're a dark golden brown.
  • Step 4: Add the garlic paste and sautÃ© for 1 minute.
  • Step 5: Add the chicken and stir till it turns opaque (no pink should be visible).
  • Step 6: Add 2 Cups of water, cover and cook for 15 minutes to tenderize chicken.
  • Step 7:Add all the whole spices to a cheesecloth and tie the ends to create a sachet.
  • Step 8: Uncover the chicken, and cook on high till all the water evaporates.
  • Step 9: Add the salt and coriander powder and saute until the onions are completely broken down and the chicken is medium brown.
  • Step 10: Strain the rice and discard the water it was soaking in.
  • Step 11: Add the rice along with the sachet of spices to the chicken, along with 3 cups of water.
  • Step 12: Cook till the water has evaporated, and sprinkle the black cumin seeds on top.
  • Step 13: Reduce the flame to the lowest setting, seal the pot with foil, place the lid on top and steam it for 15 minutes.
  • Step 14: Uncover the pot, remove the foil, and lightly fluff the rice. Remove the sachet of rice before serving.
